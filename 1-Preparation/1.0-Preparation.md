
# Preparation Phase

- [**Threat Intelligence**](./Threat-Intelligence.md): Gathering and sharing information about threats and adversaries.
- [**Hardening and Baselines**](./Hardening-and-Baselines.md): Applying security benchmarks and policies to reduce attack surface.
- [**Vulnerability Management**](./Vulnerability-Management.md): Identifying, assessing, and remediating vulnerabilities.
